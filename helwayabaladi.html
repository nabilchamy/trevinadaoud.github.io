Helwa Ya Baladi

The Egyptian Artisanal House

Bachelor Project in Lebanon

It is a building located in Downtown in Beirut and built in the same style of houses in the village of Aswan (Egypt). It would have the same feeling as a house in Aswan from its exterior and would have its own particular style in the interior with colorful walls decorated with beautiful drawings.

Inside, we would find a a modern home with a traditional sensibility. The products would be arranged in the places they would be found in a regular house. Abayas would be in the closet, curtains would be hung on window...etc.

The artisanal house would be comprised of two floors: The first floor would consist of the products for sale. The second floor would be made up of workshops divided by glass partitions for the artisans to work in.
